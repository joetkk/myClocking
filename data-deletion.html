Data Deletion Request – myClocking

myClocking respects user privacy and provides a way for users to request deletion of their personal data.

How to Request Data Deletion

To request deletion of your data, please contact us using the details below:

Email: joe@bca.com.my

Subject: Data Deletion Request – myClocking

In your request, please include:

  • Your registered username or employee ID
  • The company or organization name (if applicable)

What Data Is Deleted

Upon verification, the following data may be deleted:

  • User account information
  • Attendance records
  • Uploaded photos (if applicable)
  • Location-related records associated with attendance

Data Retention

Some data may be retained if required by:

  • Legal obligations
  • Company or organizational policies

Any retained data will be stored securely and deleted once retention requirements are met.

Processing Time

Data deletion requests are typically processed within a reasonable timeframe after verification.

If you have questions, please contact us at the email above.

Privacy Policy — myClocking

myClocking respects your privacy. This policy explains what data the app may access and how it is used.

Permissions

Camera: Used only to capture photos/selfies for attendance features when you choose to use the camera.

Location (if enabled): Used to support location/geofence-based attendance features (if your app uses location).

Data Collection & Use

The app may process the photo and/or location information you provide to deliver its features (e.g., clock-in verification).

We do not sell your personal information.

We do not share personal data with third parties except when required to operate the service or comply with law.

Data is retained only as long as necessary for the app’s functionality and your organization’s requirements.

Security
